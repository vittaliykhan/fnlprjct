About me

My name is Vitaliy but everyone calls me Vit.

One of my passions is reading books and I would like to meet more people with the same interest!

I truly believe that reading could help humanity to become better

Hello and Welcome to Vit's Book Club!

Just a quick intro: I'm Vit. I was born in Almaty, Kazakhstan. I am interested in books and stories that they can tell.

I find reading and creative activities to be a true sanctuary for me, especially when life becomes overwhelming. As someone diagnosed with Autism Spectrum Disorder, my heightened sensory sensitivity often leads to sensory overload. However, engaging in these activities allows me to escape and find respite from the sensory challenges I face.

It's wonderful to hear that fantasy books, particularly the Harry Potter series, provide you with a favorite escape. J.K. Rowling's magical world has captured the hearts of millions of readers around the globe. Being sorted into Hufflepuff on Pottermore means that you possess qualities such as loyalty, dedication, and a strong sense of fairness. Hufflepuffs are known for their inclusive nature and their ability to value and appreciate others. Embracing your Hufflepuff identity can be a source of pride and a way to connect with fellow fans of the series.

When it comes to finding solace and getting lost in a story, "chick lit" fiction is a genre I often turn to. There's something delightful about immersing myself in the lives of relatable female protagonists as they navigate love, career, friendships, and personal growth. On sunny afternoons, when I have the luxury of lounging in the garden, these light-hearted and entertaining books make for the perfect easy reads. They offer a charming escape into a world that feels both familiar and enchanting, allowing me to unwind and enjoy the sunshine while getting lost in a captivating narrative.

And then there are times when you really want some fresh new story, something you haven't read yet, something short (or not quite so short) for a change. Those are the times for discovering unpublished stories, fan fiction and similar. I've got a few of those for your delectation on this site too. I have found them from different artists and I believe they are worth reading.

Settle in and make yourself comfortable while you embark on your reading journey. Happy reading and may your literary adventures be filled with joy and relaxation!
