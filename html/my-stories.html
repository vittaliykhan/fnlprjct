About More stories

Currently, most of these writings are fan fiction. I came across fan fiction during a challenging period in my life, marked by bullying at school prior to diagnosis and various difficulties at home. Fan fiction became a lifeline for me, and I mean that quite literally. By crafting stories, I discovered a safe outlet for my emotions, allowing me to express myself while finding solace in the words I needed to hear, even if they came from a fictional character.

Happy Reading!
